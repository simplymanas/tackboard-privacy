Privacy Policy for Tackboard

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tackboard Android app (package com.simplymanas.tackboard, the "App") handles your information. The short version: the App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Tackboard does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. The App has no account system, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Where your notes are stored

Every note you create — its text and colours — is saved only in the App's private storage on your own device. This data never leaves your device. The App does not request the Internet permission and makes no network connections.

Information we share

None. Because no data is collected and nothing is transmitted off your device, there is nothing to share. The App uses no third-party services, advertising networks, or trackers.

Deleting your data

Your notes remain entirely under your control. You can delete an individual note inside the App, or remove all stored data at once by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage in your device settings.

Children's privacy

Tackboard is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this same address with a new "Last updated" date.
