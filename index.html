Privacy Policy for Tackboard

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tackboard Android app (package com.simplymanas.tackboard, the "App") handles your information. The short version: the App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. Tackboard has no account system, no sign-in, no analytics, and no ads.

Where your notes are stored

Every note — its text, colours, and any voice recording — is saved only in the App's private storage on your device. This data never leaves your device. The App does not request the Internet permission and makes no network connections.

Microphone and voice notes

Tackboard lets you record an optional voice note. To do this, the App requests the microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) permission, and only while you are actively recording. Recorded audio is saved as a file in the App's private storage on your device and is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. You can play back or delete a recording at any time; deleting the note or emptying Trash removes its audio file.

Information we share

None. Because nothing is transmitted off your device, there is nothing to share. The App uses no third-party services, advertising networks, or trackers.

Deleting your data

You can delete an individual note (and its recording) inside the App, or remove all stored data at once by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage in your device settings.

Children's privacy

Tackboard is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy
